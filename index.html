About Our Baital G. P. Vidyapith

Baital Gopeswar Pal Vidyapith is the face of this village. It was established in 1926 with the spontaneous sacrifices of some large-hearted personnel. At the very beginning its infrastructure was small and the number of learner was very little. But with the passing of time its physique is developing day after day. At the dawn of this Bhuson school, Amulya Ratan Ray, adorns the honorable seat of H.M. Now it is big enough in the aspects of its infrastructure and pupils. Its discipline, results, teaching learning activities and evaluation have raised the school to an ever blooming position in the society. The motto of the institution is to extract the hidden excellence from the minds of learners.
